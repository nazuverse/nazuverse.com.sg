Whispers Beneath the Moon

In the forest where shadows whisper low,
And where their footsteps get ahead slow,
Moonlight dances on the silver leaves’ glow,
And the river from faraway flow.

Carrying secrets only the night will know,
And by the morning everything will go,
Vanished like echoes in dawn's soft throw,
Your dream was nothing but like wind though.

Fading whispers where the cold winds blow,
Or the summer heat that hides what show,
Veiling truths beneath a golden glow,
Lies above the white snow.

Secrets buried where no footprints go,
The dead men tell not tales but a vow,
Silent oaths beneath the ancient bough,
I lie down in my bed dreaming as though.

Wandering paths where shadowed memories grow,
Pasts that will never return glow,
Flickering embers of stories untold, slow,
Whispered an eternal so.

In echoes where lost souls softly go,
Where they echo of peace, of tranquility although,
Bound to the hush of the moon’s silver glow,
Abundance that never stops to flow.

In that dream they are forever in serenity as they know.
